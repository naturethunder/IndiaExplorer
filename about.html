IndiaExplore is a travel-discovery platform built around honesty, depth and the joy of planning — covering 2,355 destinations across all 35 states & union territories.

Our Mission

Planning a trip in India is wonderful and overwhelming in equal measure. Information is scattered across a dozen tabs, prices are inconsistent, and the most beautiful places are often the hardest to find. We built IndiaExplore to fix that — to be the single, trustworthy starting point for discovering where to go, what to see, and where to stay.

Our promise is simple: thoughtful curation over endless listings. Every destination on the platform is hand-picked, described honestly, and paired with real places to visit, stays across every budget, live weather, and practical routes to reach it.

Our Story

IndiaExplore started as a personal travel notebook — a growing collection of places worth visiting, hotels worth booking, and routes worth remembering. As the list grew from a handful of weekend getaways to over a hundred destinations, it became clear that other travellers were searching for exactly the same thing: a calm, honest, well-organised guide to incredible India.

Today the platform spans the snow peaks of Ladakh and Auli, the beaches of Goa and Gokarna, the heritage of Jaipur and Hampi, the backwaters of Kerala, and the living root bridges of the North-East — each with its own detail page, interactive map, and live weather updated in real time.
