🔒 Your Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

IndiaExplore ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use this website, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using IndiaExplore you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

IndiaExplore is a static informational website. We do not require you to create an account to browse destinations, and we do not sell personal data. The information involved falls into three categories:

  • Information you provide: If you contact us through our form, you give us a name and email address. These are used only to respond to your message.
  • Information stored on your device: Preferences such as saved destinations may be kept in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Approximate location (optional): Live weather is shown for destinations, not for you. We do not track your physical location unless you explicitly allow it.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display destination details, live weather, maps and photos.
  • To remember your saved destinations and preferences on your device.
  • To respond to enquiries you send through our contact form.
  • To improve and maintain the website.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide live information, IndiaExplore loads data from a few trusted third-party services. When you view a page, your browser may contact these providers directly:

  • Open-Meteo — free weather data used to show live temperature and conditions for each destination.
  • Wikimedia Commons — supplies real, openly-licensed photographs of places.
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet — powers the interactive maps on destination pages.

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share personal data with them; requests are limited to the data needed to render weather, photos and maps.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Any data we keep (such as saved destinations) is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

IndiaExplore is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

You may clear locally-stored data at any time, decline location access, and contact us to ask what information we hold or to request its deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
